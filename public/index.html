About The Amplitude Institute

Lean Agile methodology for rapid innovation: validate, build, patent

Our Vision

Launch breakthrough innovations in weeks, not months. Validate hypotheses through customer-driven development and generate 1+ patents per 2-day sprint.

Our Mission

  • Week 1-2: Customer discovery through 20 targeted interviews
  • Week 3: Design focused 2-day innovation sprints
  • Week 4: Execute weekend pilot with 4-5 engineers
  • Day 30: Scale/Pivot decision based on validated learning

Core Activities

Lean Agile approach to validated learning and rapid IP generation

Customer Discovery

20 customer interviews with engineers and potential sponsors to validate problem assumptions through behavior-based questions.

2-Day MVP Sprints

Focused weekend innovation sprints with 4-5 engineers generating patentable concepts and rapid prototypes.

Validated Learning

Evidence-based approach: test pricing, recruit pilot participants, and capture all learnings for iteration.

Bootstrap Funding

Personal investment (<$1K), volunteer equity partners, and existing tools to minimize initial resource requirements.

Decision Gates

Built-in validation checkpoints: 70% problem confirmation, 4+ committed participants, and scale/pivot decisions.
