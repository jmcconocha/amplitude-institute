MVP Weekend Sprint: "Validate & Build"

Our signature 2-day collaborative R&D experience

Pre-Sprint: Customer Validation Complete

20 customer interviews completed confirming problem exists with 70%+ validation rate. Pilot participants recruited and committed.

Saturday: Problem Framing & Ideation

Problem review from customer interviews, rapid brainstorming, and concept development. Generate 1-2 patentable approaches with 4-5 engineers.

Sunday: Prototyping & IP Development

Rapid prototyping with minimal tools, provisional patent outline development, and systematic failure logging for future iterations.

Post-Sprint: Decision Gate

Results analysis, learnings capture, provisional patent filing (if applicable), and scale/pivot decision for next 30-day cycle.

Community Requirements
